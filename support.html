Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a deposit?

Go to the Deposit page, select your payment method, enter the amount, upload payment proof, and submit. Our team will review and approve within 24 hours.

How long do withdrawals take?

Withdrawals are processed within 24-48 hours after approval. A 2% fee is applied to all withdrawals.

How do trading bots work?

Activate a bot by investing funds. The bot trades automatically and generates profits hourly based on the expected ROI. You can claim profits anytime.

What is KYC verification?

KYC (Know Your Customer) is required for withdrawals. Upload your ID document (front and back) and we'll verify it within 24 hours.

How does the referral program work?

Share your unique referral code with friends. When they sign up, you earn $10 commission instantly added to your balance.
